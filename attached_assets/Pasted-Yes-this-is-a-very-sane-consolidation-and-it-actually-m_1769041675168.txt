## 1. First: the correct mental model (important)

Customers should not be “subscribing to agents.”

They should be subscribing to **outcomes**, where agents are **internal workers**.

So the right framing is:

* **Products / Modules** (paid or free)
* Internally powered by multiple agents

---

This is the backbone. Everything else hangs off this.

**Includes (internally):**

* SERP tracking
* Keyword movement
* Ranking deltas
* Top 3 / Top 10 counts
* SERP volatility

**Why it’s paid**

* Continuous value
* Directly tied to outcomes
* Easy to understand
* Industry-standard expectation

This is the one people *must* subscribe to.
