1. What’s Broken (and Why It Matters)

A. You’re forcing an unnecessary context switch

Right now the flow is:

Preview → “Check your email” → click link → come back → choose plan → pay

This is bad because:
	•	It breaks momentum at the exact moment of highest intent
	•	It introduces doubt (“Why can’t I just continue?”)
	•	It feels defensive, not confident
	•	It’s optimized for authentication, not conversion

For a product like Arclo, email verification should not block checkout.

⸻

B. The page doesn’t answer the question: “How do I start?”

The user sees:
	•	A great preview
	•	A “Love this preview? Claim it now.” message
	•	But no obvious primary action that says “Sign up and start”

So the brain goes:

“Okay… what do I do now?”

That moment of hesitation is deadly.

⸻

C. Header overlap is killing first impression

The sticky header still covers the top of the preview content.

That communicates:
	•	visual sloppiness
	•	lack of polish
	•	“this might break elsewhere too”

This needs to be fixed immediately — it’s table stakes.

⸻

2. Your Philosophy (Correctly Stated)

Your philosophy is:

“If someone likes what they see, they should be able to continue immediately — choose a plan, enter payment, and be live — without leaving the flow.”

That philosophy is exactly right for:
	•	SMBs
	•	Done-for-you products
	•	Preview-driven onboarding
	•	High-confidence automation tools

And yes — this is industry best practice for products like:
	•	Webflow
	•	Shopify
	•	Squarespace
	•	Framer
	•	Modern AI tools

They do not block checkout on email verification.

3. The Correct Workflow (Aligned with Your Philosophy)

Here is the ideal Arclo flow, end-to-end:

STEP 1 — Generate Preview

User enters business info → sees preview

STEP 2 — Immediate Primary CTA (Above the Fold)

Right next to the preview:

Primary CTA:

“Launch My Website”

Subtext:

Choose a plan and go live. You can change or cancel anytime.

No email gating yet.

⸻

STEP 3 — Inline Plan Selection (Modal or Side Panel)

When user clicks “Launch My Website”:
	•	Show plan selector (Grow pre-selected)
	•	Show add-ons (already built)
	•	Total price updates live

This happens in the same screen, no navigation.

⸻

STEP 4 — Checkout Immediately

Same modal/panel continues to:
	•	Email field (for account creation)
	•	Payment method (card)
	•	“Start My Subscription”

At this point:
	•	User has committed
	•	Momentum is preserved
	•	Trust is reinforced

⸻

STEP 5 — Email Verification Happens After Payment

Once payment succeeds:
	•	Show success state
	•	Send email:
“Confirm your email to manage your site”
	•	Email verification is required for:
	•	dashboard access
	•	future logins
	•	Not required to start

This is the key shift.

4. Fixing the Header Overlap (Non-Negotiable)

Correct behavior
	•	Sticky header must never cover content
	•	Page content must start below header height
	•	Especially on preview/results pages

Rule

If header is fixed:
	•	Add top padding/margin equal to header height
	•	Must be responsive (desktop vs mobile)

This should be treated as a bug, not a UX tweak.
