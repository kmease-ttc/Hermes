1. Email-Gated Preview Scan (No Account, No Payment)

Trigger:

* Email submitted (soft verification acceptable)
* Domain + email combo stored
* Cooldown enforced (e.g. 30 days per domain/email)

System behavior:

* Run **Preview Scan ONLY**
* **No SERP**
* **No paid keyword APIs**
* **No competitor discovery**

Purpose:
Prove legitimacy and accuracy **without leaking value**.

Preview Scan Capabilities (Allowed):

* Crawl ≤25 pages
* Sitemap + robots.txt
* Structural + surface signals

Sections Shown:

1. Technical Signals (Structural)

   * Pages crawled
   * Indexable vs blocked
   * Missing / duplicate titles
   * Missing H1s
   * Error pages (4xx/5xx)

2. Content Structure

   * Thin pages (word count threshold)
   * Pages without a clear topic (no H1 / vague title)

3. Performance

   * PageSpeed Insights (lab only, if available)

Presentation Rules:

* No traffic numbers
* No keyword names
* No rankings
* No competitors
* No scores like “48/100”
* Status labels only:

  * Looks OK
  * Needs Attention
  * Critical

CTA (single, clear):
“See competitors, keywords, and what to fix first → Run Full Scan”

────────────
Cost impact:
Low crawl cost only
SERP spend = $0
