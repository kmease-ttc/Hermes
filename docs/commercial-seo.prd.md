# PRD — Commercial SEO / Packaging & Pricing
(Base Plan, Add‑Ons, Hosting, Autonomy)

---

## 1. Purpose & Scope

This PRD defines **what Arclo sells, how it is packaged, and how pricing maps to value**.

It governs:
- Subscription plans and tiers
- Included features vs paid add‑ons
- Keyword limits and SERP usage
- Hosting and autonomy offerings
- Monthly vs annual pricing rules

This PRD does **not** define:
- Billing mechanics or entitlements (Billing PRD)
- Onboarding flow or paywall copy (Onboarding PRD)
- Recommendation logic (Mission Control PRD)

---

## 2. Target Customer

Primary customers:
- Small and medium‑sized businesses
- Solo founders and operators
- Teams without in‑house SEO staff

Customer goals:
- Rank higher for relevant keywords
- Increase organic traffic
- Avoid hiring agencies or specialists
- See progress with minimal effort

---

## 3. Product Philosophy

- **Loss leader first**: One free SEO report to demonstrate value
- **Simple entry point**: Low‑friction base plan
- **Pay for usage**: Expensive data (SERP, competitors) gated as add‑ons
- **Scale with confidence**: Autonomy and hosting as premium upgrades
- **Friendly pricing**: Transparent, modular, no forced bundles

---

## 4. Free Offering (Loss Leader)

### 4.1 Free SEO Report (One‑Time)

Includes:
- Keyword rankings snapshot
- Ranked vs unranked keywords
- Top 10 and #1 positions
- Missed keyword opportunities

Rules:
- Can be generated once per account
- Remains downloadable
- Marked **stale** after viewing
- Cannot be refreshed without subscription

Purpose:
- Create urgency
- Demonstrate competitive gap
- Drive conversion

---

## 5. Base Subscription (Required)

### 5.1 Base Plan Overview

The base plan is the **entry point for all paid usage**.

Includes:
- Weekly SEO reports (email + dashboard)
- SERP tracking for **25 keywords**
- Keyword ranking changes over time
- Technical SEO recommendations
- Content gap analysis
- Internal linking suggestions
- Domain authority tracking
- Exportable reports (PDF / email‑ready)

Analytics:
- Google Analytics and Search Console supported
- Optional but strongly encouraged

---

### 5.2 Base Plan Pricing

- **$149 / month**
- **$1,499 / year** (≈20% discount)

Annual pricing is shown **outside onboarding** only.

---

## 6. Keyword Packages (Paid Add‑Ons)

SERP data incurs real cost and is sold separately.

Only **one keyword tier** may be active at a time.

| Package | Total Keywords | Monthly Price |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| Included | 25 | Included |
| Tier 1 | 100 | +$20 / mo |
| Tier 2 | 250 | +$40 / mo |
| Tier 3 | 500 | +$80 / mo |

Rules:
- Upgrades take effect immediately
- Downgrades apply next billing cycle
- Hermes enforces pacing regardless of tier

---

## 7. Competitor Rankings & Snapshots (Add‑On)

### 7.1 Description

Provides:
- Automated competitor discovery
- SERP comparisons vs competitors
- Ranking deltas over time
- Snapshot history

Competitor SERP pulls incur additional cost.

---

### 7.2 Pricing

- **$49 / month** includes up to **5 competitors**
- **+$10 / month per additional competitor**

Competitors can be:
- Auto‑detected
- Manually added or removed

---

## 8. Content Grading & Blog Writing (Hemingway Add‑On)

### 8.1 Description

Includes:
- Page‑level content grading
- SEO‑aligned improvement suggestions
- Blog topic generation
- Blog draft creation

Not limited to blogs — applies to **any page**.

---

### 8.2 Pricing

- **$79–$99 / month** (final price TBD)

Limits (configurable):
- Pages graded per month
- Blog drafts per month

## 9. Hosting (Optional Add‑On)

### 9.1 Description

When enabled:
- Arclo hosts the site code
- Integrates directly with GitHub
- Enables safe autonomous publishing

Hosting is required for full autonomy.

---

### 9.2 Pricing

- **$19.99 / month**

Purpose:
- Cover infrastructure costs
- Unlock deeper automation

---

## 10. Full Autonomous Publishing (Premium Add‑On)

### 10.1 Description

When enabled:
- Arclo applies approved fixes automatically
- Changes are queued and tracked
- Progress visible in Mission Control
- Rollback supported

User experience:
- “Tell Arclo what you want”
- Arclo publishes changes by end of day

---

### 10.2 Pricing

- **$199–$249 / month**

Requires:
- Base plan
- Hosting add‑on
